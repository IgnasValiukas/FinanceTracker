About Finance Tracker

Finance Tracker is a simple and effective tool to help you monitor your income and expenses. Whether you're an individual or a small business owner, our app provides a clear view of your financial activity with minimal effort.

Our goal is to empower users to make smarter financial decisions by providing access to clean records, categorized history, and clear visualizations of their money flow.
